More About Us..

A blissful Sri Lankan Culture- the Wood Art, is a cultural tradition which if supported could reach high ends! Most of our products are hand-crafted by hardworking craftsmen with extreme talents. We as stakeholders of this amazing cultural product look forward to promote and support these hardworking villagers. Yes, even today you could spot villagers in certain part of Sri Lanka keeping this talent alive- the villagers are blessed and manage to create new designs each time!

Our main aim is to invest in these wooden crafts and help support the local villagers and bring in a good product to the market thereby maintaining the culture and tradition of wood crafts in Sri Lanka! We promise you good and quality products at an amazing price!
